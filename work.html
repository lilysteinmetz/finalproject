UX Research Co-op at Intuit

My Role

  • TurboTax Canada UX Research co-op on the XD team
  • Collaborated with designers and stakeholders to conduct research for any and all projects
  • Recruited and targeted participants for research studies and user testing
  • Synthesized and analyzed data that was collected from research
  • Presented any findings to designers and stakeholders

Projects

FMH Consolidation

Analyzed the follow-me-home's (moderated user testings) from TY22 and pinpoint any pain points or trouble areas that customers were encountering. Synthesized the findings, located any patterns, and determined the severity of each pain point. Shared a concise and succint report including all of the 2023 FMH findings that clearly communicate actionable insights.

Email Marketing

The overall business goal of this project was to improve the S2C email customer series to ensure relevancy and help a customer complete their filing more quickly. We mapped out all of the marketing emails and in product sign up flow and did a heuristic evaluation on them as well as a competitive evaluation on the in product sign up process.

Self Employed Filing Experience

Conducted a competitive analysis to gather information and ideas to further better our self-employed filing experience. Created a report that included outside research to understand our targeted audience better and presented it to designers.

Etsy Partnership

Conducted a moderated user test in order to understand the components of a self-employed worker who uses etsy. Gathered findings to support the sync of etsy with our self-employed filing experience.

Self-Employed last minute filers - FMHs

I targeted self-employed customers who were filing their taxes after the payment deadline. This included customers who may have started their taxes prior to the deadline but completed it after. The goal of this research plan was to understand what areas of friction exist for SE customers, and why SE customers have yet to finish filing their taxes. The expected outcomes of these follow me homes would be to understand pain points with the current SE TTO experience, understand why self-employed customers are filing post payment deadlines, and identify any barriers with S2C for self-employed customers.

FY24 PLU Ideal State

Conducted outside research with a competitive analysis and presented findings to stakeholders. Assisted in the redesign of 5 major components on the front doors page including the SKU cards, upsell banner, comparison chart, sub navigation, and drop down. Targeted an audience to use for A/B testing and synthesized the results for the lead designers.

E2E Wall

Mapped out the current E2E flow for DIY, A&R, FS and included step IDs for each screen to save time in the future for designers and stakeholders to have the most updated version screenshots.

Hindi Indicator Usability

Conducted A/B testing for three different design variations of the newly offered option to file taxes in Hindi. I tested the usability and navigation of switching the language and synthesized my findings.

Design Research Playbook

Created a research playbook for the design team to reference if they need any assitance with research while the lead UX Researcher is on break in between for her contract renewal.
